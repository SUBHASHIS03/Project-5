About VIVEKA SCANS & DIAGNOSTICS

VIVEKA SCANS & DIAGNOSTICS is a premier diagnostic center located in the heart of Bengaluru, opposite Dasarahalli Metro Station. Established with a vision to provide accurate, timely, and affordable diagnostic services, we have been serving the community with dedication and excellence.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest medical technology and staffed by experienced radiologists, pathologists, and technicians who ensure the highest standards of quality and precision in every test we conduct.

Our Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive range of diagnostic services using cutting-edge technology for accurate and reliable results

CT Scan

Advanced Computed Tomography scanning for detailed cross-sectional images of the body, brain, chest, abdomen, and more.

MRI Scan

High-resolution Magnetic Resonance Imaging for brain, spine, joints, and soft tissue analysis with exceptional clarity.

Ultrasound / USG

Complete ultrasound services including pregnancy scans, abdominal, pelvic, thyroid, and Doppler studies.

Digital X-Ray

High-quality digital X-ray imaging for chest, spine, limbs, and abdomen with minimal radiation exposure.

Pathology / Lab Tests

Comprehensive blood tests, biochemistry, hematology, microbiology, and hormonal assays with fast turnaround.

ECG & Echo

Cardiac diagnostics including ECG, 2D Echo, and stress tests for comprehensive heart health assessment.

Mammography

Digital mammography for breast cancer screening and diagnosis with high sensitivity and accuracy.

Bone Densitometry (DEXA)

Accurate bone density measurement to assess osteoporosis risk and bone health status.
